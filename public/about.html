About Me

I am Dr Md Suleman Tarique Khan, highly skilled and experienced Dentist with expertise in various dental procedures. I am dedicated to providing top-notch dental care to all my patients. I have completed my BDS from Dr Ziauddin Ahmad Dental College & Hospital AMU , Aligarh and completed my Junior Residentship in Periodontology and observership in Maxillofacial Surgery from Aligarh Muslim University. Concurrently I have completed various certificate courses in Oral Health from University Of Pennsylvania USA & Oral Cancer from Eastman Dental Institute, University College London, UK. I have attended various National and International Dental Conferences along with some Hands on workshop in Implantology, Basal Implant , CBCT and many more.
